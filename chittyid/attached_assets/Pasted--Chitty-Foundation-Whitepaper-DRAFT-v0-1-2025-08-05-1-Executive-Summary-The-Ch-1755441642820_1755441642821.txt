# Chitty Foundation Whitepaper

### DRAFT v0.1 · 2025‑08‑05

---

## 1 · Executive Summary

The **Chitty Foundation** stewards the development, governance, and economic fly‑wheel of **ChittyChain**—an evidence‑centric blockchain and off‑chain trust layer purpose‑built for legal, property‑tech, and financial compliance workloads.  Our mission: *“Make proof as frictionless as speech.”*

---

## 2 · Problem Statement

Courts, regulators, and enterprises lack a unified, tamper‑proof ledger that:

1. Guarantees **chain‑of‑custody** without revealing PII.
2. Scales to millions of evidence items **per day**.
3. Provides economic incentives for independent **validators & auditors**.
4. Integrates with existing SaaS and document systems via **universal IDs**.

---

## 3 · Our Solution

| Layer                      | Role                                  | Key Tech                              |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Identifier**             | Deterministic, human‑readable IDs     | **UUID v7** (v1) → Mod‑97 Base32 (v2) |
| **Off‑Chain Immutability** | 7‑day freeze & hash                   | Postgres + pgvector, IPFS CIDs        |
| **On‑Chain Anchor**        | Final settlement & dispute resolution | EVM smart contracts, Chainlink CCIP   |
| **Trust Graph**            | Dynamic reputation & risk scoring     | Neo4j + time‑decay algos              |

## 4 · Technical Architecture

```mermaid
graph TD
    subgraph User Apps
      A1(ChittyChat) --> API
      A2(Property SaaS) --> API
      A3(Law‑Firm CMS) --> API
    end
    API --> OffChain(DB & IPFS)
    OffChain --> Oracle(Chain Oracle)
    Oracle --> Chain(Smart Contract)
    Chain --> Explorer(Proof Explorer)
    Chain -. trustScore .-> Graph(Trust Graph)
```

* **Latency Budget**: Create→Freeze < 300 ms; Freeze→Mint ≤ 5 min batch.
* **Scalability**: 100 TPS minting on L2 roll‑ups (zkSync / Arbitrum Orbit).

---

## 5 · Economic Model

### 5.1 Token (CHITTY)

* **Fixed supply**: 1 B tokens; 40 % Treasury, 30 % Validator rewards, 20 % Ecosystem grants, 10 % Team & Advisors.
* **Utility**:
   – Pay minting & storage fees.
   – Stake to become validator or oracle.
   – Vote in DAO governance once unlocked.

### 5.2 Revenue Streams

1. **Minting Fees** – 0.1 USDC ≃ 0.1 CHITTY per evidence token.
2. **Verification‑as‑a‑Service** – tiered API calls priced in USDC or CHITTY.
3. **Graph‑Analytics SaaS** – subscription for risk dashboards.
4. **IPFS Pinning** – usage‑based GB fees.
5. **Treasury Staking Yield** – treasury CHITTY allocated to liquidity pools.

## 8 · Security Model

* **Dual‑Signatures** – foundation key + validator key required for mint.
* **Slashing** – incorrect oracle data loses stake (Phase 2+).
* **Proof‑of‑Inclusion** – Merkle proof stored on‑chain; off‑chain data lives on IPFS / Postgres.
* **Audit Partners** – annual smart‑contract audit; quarterly financial audit.

---

## 9 · Compliance

* **GDPR & CCPA** – PII stored off‑chain; right‑to‑erasure removes raw data but hash remains.
* **SOC 2 Type II** – target attestation by Q1 2026.
* **ISO/IEC 27001** – ISMS certification roadmap in progress.
