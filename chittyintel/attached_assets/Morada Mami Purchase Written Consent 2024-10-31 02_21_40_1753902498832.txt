THE MEMBERS OF
ARIBIA, LLC
The undersigned, being all of the Members and Managers of ARIBIA, LLC, a Illinois limited
liability company (the "Company"), acting by written consent without a meeting pursuant to the
Illinois Limited Liability Company Act, do hereby consent to the adoption of the following and
the taking of the actions described herein, and direct that this Written Consent be filed in the
Company’s records:
WHEREAS, the members have determined it is in the best interest of the Company to
authorize the Company’s members, to the fullest extent possible, to enter into transactions in the
name of, and bind, the Company.
NOW THEREFORE LET IT BE:
RESOLVED, that the Company is authorized to purchase, acquire, maintain, manage,
operate, sell, transfer and otherwise dispose of any property, including real estate property, in
any city, town or other jurisdiction in the Republic of Colombia pursuant to, and in accordance
with, terms as determined by the Company’s Members or Manager in their sole discretion.
